Premium Motor Spirit (Petrol) Price Watch (Jan 2016 - Feb 2017)

Average price paid by consumers for automotive gas oil (diesel) increased by 3.68% month-on-month and 68.74% year-on-year to N249.38 in February 2017 from N240.52 in January 2017. States with the highest average price of diesel were Adamawa (N280), Ebonyi (N272.5) & Cross Borno (271) . States with the lowest average price of diesel were Oyo (N226.67), Bauchi (N226) and Osun (N220).

Data source: National Bureau of Statistics Nigeria
